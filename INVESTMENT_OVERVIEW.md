# 💼 Investment Overview — POS Ledger NG

> **Building the Financial Operating Platform for POS Agents and Small Businesses**

---

# Executive Summary

POS Ledger NG is an early-stage financial technology (FinTech) startup developing a digital financial management platform for POS agents, mobile money operators, and small businesses in Nigeria.

The platform helps businesses replace manual bookkeeping with structured digital transaction management, automated calculations, and financial reporting.

Our long-term vision is to become the operating platform that powers agency banking businesses and micro-enterprises across Africa.

---

# The Problem

Millions of small businesses and POS agents continue to manage their operations manually.

Common challenges include:

- Poor transaction record keeping
- Manual calculation errors
- Cash reconciliation challenges
- Missing financial records
- Limited business insights
- Difficulty preparing financial reports
- Poor tax readiness
- Lack of affordable financial management tools

These challenges reduce profitability, increase operational risks, and slow business growth.

---

# Our Solution

POS Ledger NG provides a practical digital solution that enables users to:

- Record deposits and withdrawals
- Automatically calculate transaction charges
- Monitor daily and monthly performance
- Generate business reports
- Export financial records
- Improve accountability
- Prepare tax-ready financial information

The current product is available as a desktop MVP built with Python and SQLite.

---
