# Introduction (30 seconds)

Good day everyone.

My name is Mukhtar Aliyu, founder of POS Ledger NG.

POS Ledger NG is a financial management platform built specifically for POS agents, mobile money operators, and small businesses.

Our mission is to replace manual bookkeeping with a simple digital solution that improves financial accountability, cash management, and business growth.

---

# The Problem (45 seconds)

Across Nigeria, thousands of POS agents still record transactions in notebooks or rely on memory.

This creates several problems:

- Lost transaction records
- Manual calculation errors
- Poor profit tracking
- Cash reconciliation challenges
- Limited business insights
- Difficulty preparing financial reports
- Poor tax readiness

These issues reduce efficiency and make it difficult for businesses to grow.

---

# Our Solution (1 minute)

POS Ledger NG digitizes daily financial operations.

The platform allows users to:

- Record deposits
- Record withdrawals
- Automatically calculate transaction charges
- Track daily and monthly profits
- Monitor cash flow
- Generate business reports
- Export financial records for auditing and tax preparation

This helps businesses make informed decisions based on accurate financial data.

---

# Product Walkthrough (2 minutes)

### Step 1

Open the application.

The dashboard provides access to transaction management and reporting tools.

### Step 2

Record a transaction.

Enter:

- Transaction type
- Amount
- Date
- Time

The system automatically calculates service charges based on predefined pricing rules.

### Step 3

Save the transaction.

The information is securely stored in the SQLite database.

### Step 4

Generate reports.

Users can instantly view:

- Daily summaries
- Monthly summaries
- Total deposits
- Total withdrawals
- Total charges
- Business profit

### Step 5

Export reports.

Financial reports can be exported as CSV files for auditing, business analysis, and tax preparation.
