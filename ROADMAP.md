# 🗺️ POS Ledger NG Roadmap

> **Building the future operating platform for POS agents and small businesses in Africa.**

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of POS Ledger NG. It provides transparency into our development priorities while recognizing that timelines and features may change based on user feedback, partnerships, regulatory requirements, and available resources.

---

# 🎯 Product Vision

Our goal is to build a secure, intelligent, and accessible financial operations platform that enables POS agents, mobile money operators, and small businesses to manage their businesses more efficiently.

---

# 🚀 Current Stage

### Completed

- Desktop application
- Transaction recording
- Deposit tracking
- Withdrawal tracking
- Automatic service charge calculation
- Daily financial summaries
- Monthly financial summaries
- CSV report export
- SQLite local database
- Modular Python architecture
- GitHub documentation
- MIT License

**Status:** ✅ Completed
