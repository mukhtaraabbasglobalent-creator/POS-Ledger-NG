# Business Model – POS Ledger NG

## Overview

POS Ledger NG is a FinTech platform designed to help POS agents, mobile money operators, and small businesses manage their daily financial operations through digital bookkeeping, automated reporting, and business analytics.

The platform is designed to improve financial transparency, reduce bookkeeping errors, and support financial inclusion across Nigeria and Africa.

---

# Customer Segments

Our target customers include:

- Independent POS Agents
- Agency Banking Networks
- Mobile Money Operators
- Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)
- Cooperative Societies
- Retail Stores
- Business Centers
- Financial Service Providers

# Value Proposition

POS Ledger NG enables businesses to:

- Digitize transaction records
- Reduce manual bookkeeping
- Track profits automatically
- Monitor cash flow
- Generate business reports
- Prepare tax-ready financial records
- Improve operational efficiency
- Make better business decisions using financial data
