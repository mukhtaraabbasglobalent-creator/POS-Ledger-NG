# 📘 Business Plan — POS Ledger NG

> **Building the Financial Operating Platform for POS Agents and Small Businesses**

---

# Executive Summary

POS Ledger NG is an early-stage financial technology (FinTech) startup developing a financial operations platform for POS agents, mobile money operators, and small businesses in Nigeria.

The platform replaces manual bookkeeping with a digital solution that helps users record transactions, calculate service charges automatically, monitor cash flow, and generate financial reports.

The current product is a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) built with Python and SQLite. Our long-term vision is to become a trusted financial operating platform for agency banking businesses and micro-enterprises across Africa.

# Problem Statement

Many POS agents and small businesses still rely on notebooks and manual calculations to manage daily operations.

Common challenges include:

- Poor record keeping
- Calculation errors
- Cash reconciliation problems
- Missing business records
- Limited financial insights
- Difficulty preparing tax reports
- Time-consuming bookkeeping

These issues reduce productivity and limit business growth.

---

# Our Solution

POS Ledger NG provides a digital financial management platform that enables businesses to:

- Record deposits and withdrawals
- Automatically calculate transaction charges
- Track cash balances
- Monitor profits
- Generate daily and monthly reports
- Export CSV reports
- Improve accountability
- Maintain tax-ready records
