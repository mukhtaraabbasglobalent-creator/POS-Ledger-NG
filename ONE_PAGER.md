# POS Ledger NG — One Pager

## Startup Overview

**POS Ledger NG** is an early-stage financial technology (FinTech) platform designed to help POS agents, mobile money operators, and small businesses manage their daily financial operations through digital record keeping, automated calculations, and business reporting.

The platform replaces manual bookkeeping with a structured, easy-to-use system that improves financial accountability and operational efficiency.

Current Product Stage: **Minimum Viable Product (MVP)**

---

# The Problem

Many POS agents and small businesses still rely on notebooks and manual calculations to manage their finances.

This often leads to:

- Lost transaction records
- Manual calculation errors
- Poor cash reconciliation
- Limited visibility into profits
- Difficulty preparing reports
- Poor tax readiness

These challenges reduce productivity and business growth.

---

# Our Solution

POS Ledger NG enables businesses to:

- Record deposits and withdrawals
- Automatically calculate service charges
- Monitor cash flow
- Track daily and monthly profits
- Generate financial reports
- Export CSV reports
- Maintain tax-ready financial records
